Fully Automated Level - Explanation

In the Fully Automated Level, the AI will process 3 invoices and independently decide whether to submit or decline them. You don't need to take any action, but you can view the invoices while the AI is working on them.

How it works:

  • You will see two documents: the purchase order on the left and the invoice on the right, but you cannot interact with them.
  • The AI handles the entire process automatically.
  • While the AI is processing an invoice, you can view the invoices.
