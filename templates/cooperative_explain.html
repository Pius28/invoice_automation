Cooperative Level - Explanation

In the Cooperative Level, you will process 3 invoices. The AI performs a preliminary check and provides you with a list of potential issues. Your task is to verify whether the AI has identified all errors correctly. If some issues are missing or incorrectly flagged, you can send a message, and the AI will reanalyze the invoice by comparing it with the corresponding purchase order.

How it works:

  • You will see two documents: the purchase order on the left and the invoice on the right.
  • A list of potential issues detected by the AI will also be displayed.
  • Review the list to ensure its accuracy.
  • If everything is correct, click the Submit button on the first question.
  • If there are mistakes or missing issues, provide corrections by sending a message to the AI.
  • If you believe the AI cannot assist further or the invoice contains critical errors, click the Decline button in the second question.
  • Click the "Report Issue" button to document the issue and add a correction and/or a note.
  • Based on the severity of the issues, decide whether the invoice should be submitted or declined.
  • Every identified issue must be recorded.

Basic invoice requirements:

The following elements are essential for an invoice to be valid:

  • Full name of the issuer
  • Order ID
  • Order Date
  • Quantity and description of delivered products

If any of these details are missing, the invoice must be declined.

Handling minor issues:

Minor mistakes, such as:

  • Typographical errors
  • Swapped digits

can be corrected, and the invoice may still be submitted.

Critical issues requiring rejection:

The following issues must result in invoice rejection:

  • Missing essential details as listed above
  • Incorrect total price

Beyond these rules, it is your responsibility to decide whether an invoice should be submitted or declined.
