Supervisory Control Level - Explanation

In the Supervisory Control Level, the AI will process 3 invoices.
If the AI is unsure whether to submit or decline an invoice, it will forward it to you for review.
Your task is to check the invoice and provide a note explaining your decision to submit or decline it.

How it works:

  • You will see two documents: the purchase order on the left and the invoice on the right. However, you cannot interact with them initially.
  • The AI will determine whether it can handle the invoice on its own or if it requires your input.
  • If the AI is unsure whether to submit or decline the invoice, it will activate your review screen.
  • You will see a list of detected issues along with suggested corrections.
  • Review the issue list and the invoice, then decide whether to submit or decline.
  • Provide a brief note explaining your decision.
  • Every identified issue must be recorded.

Basic invoice requirements:

The following elements are essential for an invoice to be valid:

  • Full name of the issuer
  • Order ID
  • Order Date
  • Quantity and description of delivered products

If any of these details are missing, the invoice must be declined.

Handling minor issues:

Minor mistakes, such as:

  • Typographical errors
  • Swapped digits

can be corrected, and the invoice may still be submitted.

Critical issues requiring rejection:

The following issues must result in invoice rejection:

  • Missing essential details as listed above
  • Incorrect total price

Beyond these rules, it is your responsibility to decide whether an invoice should be submitted or declined.
